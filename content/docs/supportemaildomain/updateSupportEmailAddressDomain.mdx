description: >-
  This API updates the subdomain (the "~mycompany~" part) of the support email
  address.!!1. Only the primary contact of the organization can update the
  subdomain of the support email address.!2. The new support email address will
  be support@zylker.com.!3. The updated support email address will be used for
  fetching emails, henceforth. So make sure that you update the new address in
  the forwarding configurations of your mailbox.!4. The new support email
  address is applicable only for the default department.!5. All existing email
  aliases along with the old support email address will be permanently deleted
  from your account.<br/>6. Emails forwarded to the old support address will not
  be fetched in.
